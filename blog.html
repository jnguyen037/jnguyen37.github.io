Phase 1

Objective: Players will solve a series of puzzles and riddles to find items located throughout the game. These items will either be a power up for the player or allow players to advance to the next level. Complete all the levels to win the game!

Introduction/Rules: Framed is a 2D puzzle mystery game. The protagonist, Tora, has been framed and imprisoned by an unknown protagonist for committing a serious crime. As a result our protagonists is now locked up in Alcatraz. Help him escape and prove his innocence. Complete each level and uncover the identity of the unknown protagonist. Players begin with three lives and must use the clues from the other inmates to escape. Some of the inmates are lying and others are telling the truth. Guards block your path but can be avoided or eliminated with power up items found on the map. Being caught by the guards will also decrease your lives by one. Completing each level of the first try will reward players with one extra life. Although, a player can have no more than 4 lives at any point. Failure to complete the level will decrease player lives by one and reset the level. Once the player loses all their lives, the game is over. If the antagonist if discovered the game is won.
